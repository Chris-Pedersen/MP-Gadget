double get_drift_factor ( inttime_t  ti0,
inttime_t  ti1 
)

This function integrates the cosmological prefactor for a drift step between ti0 and ti1. The value returned is

\[ \int_{a_0}^{a_1} \frac{{\rm d}a}{H(a) a^3} \]

A lookup-table is used for reasons of speed.
